Read Chinese anywhere on your Mac.

Hover any Chinese word for an instant definition, select a region for a full translation, or highlight text for a tone-colored breakdown. Works in any app. Everything runs locally. No accounts, no tracking, works offline.

Free · macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later · Apple Silicon & Intel

Hover mode.

Pick a region, the whole screen, or a specific window as your source. Move your cursor across any Chinese text and HanziKey highlights each word on screen, showing its pinyin and definition in a floating panel.

⌘⇧I Best for web pages, e-books, and anywhere you read at your own pace.

Passage mode.

Drag-select a region and HanziKey returns the full English translation, with a word-by-word breakdown underneath. Turn on auto-rescan for live subtitles or anything that updates on screen.

⌘⇧O Best for video subtitles, screenshots, and foreign-language UI.

Text-select mode.

Highlight Chinese in any app, press the hotkey, and HanziKey returns a tone-colored breakdown with pinyin, CC-CEDICT definitions, and the full English translation. No OCR, just instant lookup of whatever you can select.

⌘⇧Y Best for iMessage, Slack, copy-pasteable PDFs, and search results.

100% on-device.

OCR runs through Apple's Vision framework. Translations go through Apple's on-device Translate model with the language pack downloaded once. The CC-CEDICT dictionary ships with the app.

  • No accounts, no servers, no cloud sync.
  • No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking.
  • Captured screen pixels are discarded the moment OCR finishes.
