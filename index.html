Four ways to read Chinese.

HanziKey lives in your menu bar and reaches into any app on your Mac — browsers, PDFs, video subtitles, foreign-language software UIs.

⌖

Hover Dictionary

⌘⇧I Pick a source — region, screen, or window — then hover any Chinese word for instant pinyin + definitions.

☰

Translate Region

⌘⇧O Drag-select a paragraph anywhere on screen. See the full translation plus a word-by-word breakdown.

↩

Selection Lookup

⌘⇧Y Select Chinese in any app, press the hotkey. Pinyin, tone-colored characters, and CC-CEDICT definitions.

阿

Pinyin Conversion

⌘⇧P Turn numbered pinyin (ni3 hao3) into tone marks (nǐ hǎo) in any text field.

Built for privacy.

HanziKey uses Apple's on-device Vision framework for OCR and Apple Translate for sentence-level translations. Nothing about what you read leaves your Mac.

●No accounts. No login. No cloud sync.
●No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting.
●Captured screen regions are processed on-device and discarded immediately.
●CC-CEDICT dictionary ships with the app — works offline.
